About Me

I'm a web developer, writer, gamer, and unabashed nerd from New Hampshire. I know HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and am familiar with Bootstrap, AJAX, and Sass. I believe the key to creating good content is understanding what makes content good: not just how it works, but why.

My hobbies include reading, weight-lifting, and playing XCOM for the 208th time. I really like to talk about '50s sci-fi movies and Battlestar Galactica. If you would like to talk to me about '50s sci-fi movies and Battlestar Galactica–or, of course, web development–feel free to contact me.
